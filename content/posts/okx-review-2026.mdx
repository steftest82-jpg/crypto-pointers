<p>OKX has quietly become one of the most feature-rich cryptocurrency exchanges in the world. Ranking consistently in the top five by trading volume, the platform combines institutional-grade trading tools with consumer-friendly features like copy trading and a multi-chain Web3 wallet. In 2026, OKX processes over $8 billion in daily volume.</p>

<p>This review covers everything from fee structures to the Web3 wallet to help you decide whether OKX belongs in your exchange lineup. Note that OKX is not available to US residents, so this review is aimed at international traders.</p>

<h2 id="company-background">Company Background</h2>

<p><a href="https://www.okx.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OKX</a> was founded in 2017 and is headquartered in Seychelles with major offices in Dubai, Hong Kong, and Singapore. The exchange serves over 50 million users across 180 countries. OKX holds regulatory licenses in Dubai, the Bahamas, and several other jurisdictions.</p>

<p>The platform was formerly known as OKEx and rebranded to OKX in 2022. Since the rebrand, the company has invested heavily in its Web3 infrastructure and expanded its derivatives offerings. OKX sponsors major sports teams and events globally as part of its brand-building strategy.</p>

<p>OKX publishes monthly proof-of-reserves reports that verify the exchange holds customer funds on a 1:1 basis. The reports cover Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, and other major assets. This transparency initiative was one of the first among top-tier exchanges and has been maintained consistently since late 2022.</p>

<h2 id="trading-fees">Trading Fees and Products</h2>

<p>OKX offers some of the lowest trading fees among major exchanges. Spot trading starts at 0.08 percent for makers and 0.1 percent for takers. Holding OKB, the platform's native token, reduces fees by an additional 20 percent. These rates undercut most competitors including Binance and Coinbase.</p>

<p>The derivatives suite includes USDT perpetual contracts, coin-margined perpetuals, futures with expiry dates, and options. Leverage goes up to 125x on BTC perpetuals, though most traders use far less. Derivatives fees start at 0.02 percent maker and 0.05 percent taker, competitive with <a href="/blog/bybit-vs-kraken-2026">Bybit</a>.</p>

<p>Spot trading supports over 350 tokens with deep liquidity on major pairs. The advanced trading interface includes TradingView charts, order book visualization, and customizable layouts. OKX also offers a simplified trading mode for less experienced users who want a clean buying experience.</p>

<h2 id="copy-trading">Copy Trading Platform</h2>

<p>OKX's copy trading feature lets you automatically replicate the trades of successful traders. You can browse top traders by their return percentage, win rate, risk score, and follower count. Once you follow a trader, your account executes the same positions proportionally to your allocated capital.</p>

<p>The platform takes a 10 percent profit-sharing fee from copy trading gains, paid to the lead trader. There are no additional fees beyond the standard trading costs. You can set maximum position sizes and stop-loss limits to control your risk exposure independently of the lead trader's strategy.</p>

<p>Copy trading is available for both spot and derivatives markets. The feature is particularly popular among newer traders who want exposure to professional strategies while learning the market. However, past performance does not guarantee future returns, and copy trading still carries all the risks of regular trading.</p>

<h2 id="web3-wallet">Web3 Wallet and DeFi</h2>

<p>The OKX Web3 Wallet is a non-custodial multi-chain wallet built directly into the OKX app and browser extension. It supports over 80 blockchains including Ethereum, Solana, Bitcoin, and all major EVM chains. The wallet includes a DEX aggregator that routes swaps across multiple decentralized exchanges for the best price.</p>

<p>Cross-chain bridging is integrated into the wallet, letting you move assets between chains without visiting external bridge protocols. This reduces the risk of phishing from fake bridge sites. Gas fees are clearly displayed before every transaction with no hidden markups.</p>

<p>The wallet also includes an NFT marketplace aggregator, a DeFi earnings dashboard, and inscription support for Bitcoin Ordinals. For traders who want a single app covering centralized exchange, DeFi, and NFTs, OKX provides the most comprehensive package available. For dedicated DeFi wallets, compare with our <a href="/blog/metamask-vs-phantom-2026">MetaMask vs Phantom guide</a>.</p>

<h2 id="safety-regulation">Safety and Regulation</h2>

<p>OKX maintains cold storage for 95 percent of user funds and publishes regular proof-of-reserves audits. The exchange uses multi-signature wallets, withdrawal whitelisting, and advanced anti-phishing protections. There have been no major security breaches since the platform's launch.</p>

<p>Regulatory status varies by jurisdiction. OKX holds a provisional Virtual Asset Service Provider license in Dubai and is registered in the Bahamas. The exchange is not available in the United States, United Kingdom, or certain other restricted jurisdictions. Always verify local availability before creating an account.</p>

<h2 id="faq">FAQ</h2>

<h3>Can US residents use OKX?</h3>
<p>No, OKX does not serve US residents due to regulatory restrictions. US-based traders should consider regulated alternatives like Coinbase, Kraken, or Gemini. Using a VPN to access restricted exchanges violates their terms of service and risks account freezing or fund seizure.</p>

<h3>How does OKX compare to Binance?</h3>
<p>OKX offers slightly lower maker fees at 0.08 percent versus Binance's 0.1 percent. Binance has a larger selection of listed tokens and higher overall liquidity. OKX's Web3 wallet and copy trading features are more polished than Binance's equivalents. Both are solid choices for international traders.</p>

<h3>Is the OKX Web3 Wallet safe?</h3>
<p>The OKX Web3 Wallet is non-custodial, meaning you control your private keys. Your funds are secured by your seed phrase and device encryption, not by OKX's servers. The wallet has been audited by multiple security firms and supports hardware wallet integration for additional protection through <a href="https://www.coingecko.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Ledger devices</a>.</p>
